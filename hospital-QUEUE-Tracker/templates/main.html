About City Hospital

City Hospital is a modern multi-speciality healthcare institution dedicated to providing high-quality medical services with compassion, care, and advanced technology. Established with the vision of delivering affordable and accessible healthcare to all sections of society. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced diagnostic facilities, and highly experienced doctors across multiple departments. Patient care is our highest priority. Our doctors and medical staff work round the clock to ensure quality service. We have implemented a Smart Hospital Queue and Wait-Time Prediction System to help patients plan their visit efficiently.
